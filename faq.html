Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your games made?

Time4cornhole games are made in Beaumont, CA.

Are your games regulation?

Yes! Time4cornhole games are made to tournament specifications. Tournament games are 24" x 48", 6" in hole, 9" from the top, 4" high at the front of the game, 12" High at the back.

Are your bags regulation?

Yes! All bags are made with Duck cloth, 6" x 6", Triple Stitched, Filled with whole Corn, each bag is is weighed at 16 oz. Our bags are filled with corn that's why it called Cornhole!

Can your games stay outside?

We recommend that you put your games away at night after a fun day of play as our game is a wooden product.

Do you ship?

Yes! We ship anywhere in the U.S.
