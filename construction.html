Game Construction

Handmade in America!

Here at Time4Cornhole we take pride in each and every game made. Our games are made of 3/4" quality Birch plywood, inlaid in a pine frame. All games are pocket screwed and glued for strength and durability. Each game has a side handle for easy carrying and comfort. Time4Cornhole's signature wood grain games are hand painted with 3 coats of Varathane varnish for beautiful shine and luster.

Game Dimensions:
Tournament games 24" x 48"
Tailgate games 24" x 36"
Travel games 12" x 24"
