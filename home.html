Time4Cornhole is a Southern California based business that prides itself on quality. Our games are beautiful and handmade, but best of all: fun! Cornhole, also known as bean bag toss, corn toss, baggo or bags, is a lawn game in which players take turns throwing corn hole bags at a raised platform with a hole in the far end. Order one of our cornhole games to take on camping trips, beach trips, family reunions, parties, or even for relaxed backyard play with family and friends.
